Theater redesign for a Florida client under extreme time pressure. Brought in to replace the original designer, we needed fast design direction approval without weeks of traditional rendering.

## Approach

I developed AI-assisted concept sections using ByteDance and NanoBanana to rapidly explore material palettes. Working from Rhino section cuts, I generated collage-style visualizations with intentionally sketchy, cut-paper aesthetics. Two options presented: blue-black-copper and burgundy-wood. Client selected the wood scheme.

## Workflow

Rhino sections were fed to AI models with reference images and LLM-generated prompts. This enabled rapid iteration during crunch time while maintaining spatial and material clarity.
